Overview

Any users who are members of PO approval team can approve the purchase. Without every members approval a purchase cannot be confirmed. However, the team lead of that approval team can directly confirm the purchase order even if any member's approval state is pending.

Features

> Purchase approval team creation with team lead and members.
> Cannot Confirm the purchase without all members approval.
> In case of emergency team lead can confirm PO without all members approval.
